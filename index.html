Our Story

Built on trust, guided by compassion

HealthFirst Clinic opened its doors in Juba in 2012 with one goal: make quality healthcare genuinely accessible to every South Sudanese family, regardless of means or background.

Over twelve years we have grown from a four-room consultation centre to a full-service facility with an in-patient ward, modern laboratory, 24-hour emergency unit, and a team of 18 specialist physicians.

"Our mission is to deliver patient-centred care that restores health and rebuilds lives — with skill, empathy, and respect for every person who walks through our doors."

🎯 Mission

Accessible, affordable, and high-quality healthcare for all communities in South Sudan.

🌟 Vision

A healthier South Sudan where no one is denied care due to geography or poverty.

🤝 Values

Integrity, compassion, excellence, and community partnership drive every decision.

🏅 Accreditation

Licensed by the South Sudan Ministry of Health; WHO-aligned clinical protocols.
